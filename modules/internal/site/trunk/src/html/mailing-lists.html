Mailing Lists

Google Groups hosts the quokka mailing lists. The lists can be accessed in several ways:

  • Anonymous browsing of the lists online
  • Via email, like a traditional mailing list
  • If you have a google account, you can post messages online and configure the frequency mails are sent to you. e.g. a daily summary mail.

At present, there are 2 mailing lists, quokka-dev and quokka-user. The dev mailing list is intended for quokka committers only. The user list is the place to ask your questions.
